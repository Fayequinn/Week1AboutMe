My Favourite Things To Do

As I mentioned earlier, In our spare time as a family we enjoy going away in our motorhome and taking the paddleboards to new places. We have spent both summer's exploring the beautiful country we live in. In 2021 we went to the Pembrokeshire coast for 10 days, travelling around and visiting some amazing spots. This is the year we also discovered Bala Lake in Wales. It's the perfect place for paddleboard beginner's! The site backs on to the lake, so once you are set up you can walk to the Lake and enjoy a full weekend without the hassel of travellong each day. Since we have had the van we have been to lots of different places, below is a table of my top 5! Last summer a group of 6 families went to Talybont and the beach was amazing
